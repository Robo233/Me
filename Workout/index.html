What is calistenics

Calisthenics, also known as street workout is a physical activity performed mostly in outdoor parks or public facilities. It became a popular movement and it has now spread across the world. It's becoming a popular sport. There are also competitions organized.

Basics

There are easier and harder exercises. If you want to do the hard ones you first need to do the easy(basic) exercises. There are a few of them, like squats, push-ups or australian pull ups and there are the harder ones like regular pull-ups, dips or pistol squats.

Advanced skills

If you master the basic exercises you can move to harder ones, like muscle ups, handtstand-pushups or front levers. If you become familiar with these then there are the really advanced skills, that only a few people can do, like planche or one arm muscle ups. These are really hard to achieve, but they look really cool.

Weighted

You can use weights to make the exercises harder. Weights usually used at exercises like pull-ups or dips but even at muscle ups.
