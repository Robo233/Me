Super Burger
Description If you want to eat something delicious,try out this burger.It's less than 20 minutes to make it and it's very good!
Ingredients
  • olive oil
  • one burger bun
  • one beef burger
  • cheese
  • tomatoe
  • onion
  • cucumber
  • lettuce
Directions Step 1
Put some olive oil in the pan,heat it and put the beef in the pan, cook it for about 10 minutes,the put some cheese in it, nad wait until it's melted.

Step 2
Slice the onion, tomatoe, cucumber and letucce then put it on the burger bun. After the beef is ready put it on the vegetables. You can eat this with souces like ketchup if you want.
